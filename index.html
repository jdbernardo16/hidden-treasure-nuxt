What Is Consignment? A Complete Guide to Selling Smarter

Consignment is a powerful, low-risk method to sell your valuable items without the hassle of listing, negotiating, or shipping things yourself. Instead of managing the sale process on your own, you partner with a trusted agency like Hidden Treasures. You maintain ownership of your item until it sells, and once it does, you receive a percentage of the final sale price, while we retain a small commission for handling the entire process. This approach is popular for a reason: it’s efficient, professional, and ideal for people who want to sell luxury items or collectibles without spending time managing sales platforms or dealing with buyers directly.
